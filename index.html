What it is

A practical, informal day for policy people

Policy Camp brings together policy practitioners from across and around government to discuss the topics important to them, share best practice, and work through common frustrations in a safe, informal setting.

It is based on the UK GovCamp model: open, collaborative, participant-led, and shaped by the people who turn up.

Format

How an unconference works

Agenda created on the day

There is no fixed speaker programme. Participants suggest sessions at the start of the day.

Discuss what matters to you

Sessions are shaped around the questions, challenges, and ideas people bring with them.

Open and collaborative

You can propose a topic, join a discussion, share experience, or simply listen and learn.

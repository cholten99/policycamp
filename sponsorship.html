Become a sponsor

Policy Camp is a free, one-day unconference for people who want to make policymaking in government even better — modelled on the highly successful UK Gov Camp. Events like this only happen with the support of sponsors.

The event takes place on 23 July 2026 at University College London, with 150–200 attendees drawn from the policy profession, academia, think tanks, and the third sector.

How the money is used

Run on a not-for-profit basis

Sponsorship monies cover all event costs — venue hire at UCL, catering, AV facilities, and any attendee bursaries. We use the TransformGov bank account for payments, keeping finances clearly separate.

Any underspend is invested directly into future Policy Camp events.
